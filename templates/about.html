What is CycloHelp?

Cyclohelp is website which predict intensity of an cyclone using satellite images by using CNN This website consists of two main pages Home page and emergency page . In home page when image is uploaded with the help of CNN it predicts the intensity of cyclone When intensity of cyclone is more than a particular range in emergency page it raise an alert by sending email to people of particular area where cyclone can have adverse impact.
